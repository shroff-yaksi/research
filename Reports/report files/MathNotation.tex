\chapter*{MATHEMATICAL NOTATION}

The following symbols are used throughout this thesis. Readers following the formal derivations in Chapter~2 may use this as a quick reference.

\vspace{0.5em}
\begin{tabbing}
\textbf{Symbol} \hspace{2.5cm} \= \textbf{Meaning} \\[0.4em]
$\mathcal{D}$ \> Training dataset: all rows and their class labels \\
$\mathbf{x}$ \> A single data row (feature vector) \\
$y \in \{0, 1\}$ \> Class label (1 = minority event, 0 = majority) \\
$N$ \> Total number of rows in the dataset \\
$N_1$ \> Count of minority class rows \\
$\rho = N_1/N$ \> Minority ratio: fraction of minority rows \\
$\pi = P(y=1)$ \> Target minority ratio specified at generation time \\
$\mathcal{X}$ \> Feature space \\
$d_{num}$, $d_{cat}$ \> Number of numerical and categorical features \\
$\mathcal{C}_j$ \> Categorical domain for feature $j$ \\
$G_\theta$ \> Generative model parameterised by $\theta$ \\
$G$, $D$ \> Generator and discriminator networks in a GAN \\
$\mathcal{L}_{GAN}$ \> GAN minimax objective \\
$p_{data}(\mathbf{x})$ \> True data distribution \\
$p_\theta(\mathbf{x})$ \> Model's learned distribution \\
$q_\phi(\mathbf{z}|\mathbf{x})$ \> VAE encoder distribution parameterised by $\phi$ \\
$p_\theta(\mathbf{x}|\mathbf{z})$ \> VAE decoder distribution parameterised by $\theta$ \\
$\phi$ \> Encoder network parameters \\
$\mathbf{z}$ \> Latent vector produced by the VAE encoder \\
$d_z = 64$ \> Dimensionality of the latent space \\
$\boldsymbol{\mu}$, $\boldsymbol{\sigma}^2$ \> Mean and variance output by the VAE encoder \\
$\boldsymbol{\epsilon}$ \> Random Gaussian noise (reparameterisation or diffusion) \\
$\beta_{\text{VAE}}$ \> KL weight in the VAE loss (set to 0.1) \\
$t$ \> Diffusion timestep \\
$\mathbf{z}_t$ \> Noisy latent vector at timestep $t$ \\
$T = 1000$ \> Total number of diffusion timesteps \\
$\beta_t$ \> Noise variance added at timestep $t$ (linearly increasing) \\
$\alpha_t = 1 - \beta_t$ \> Signal retention at timestep $t$ \\
$\bar{\alpha}_t$ \> Cumulative signal retention factor at timestep $t$ \\
$\epsilon_\theta$ \> DiT noise prediction network parameterised by $\theta$ \\
$w$ \> CFG guidance scale (default 2.0) \\
$p_{uncond}$ \> Label dropout probability during CFG training (0.10) \\
$\emptyset$ \> Null token used when class label is dropped \\
$\tilde{\epsilon}$ \> Guided noise prediction (CFG output) \\
$\mathcal{L}_{VAE}$ \> Total VAE training loss \\
$\mathcal{L}_{recon}$ \> Reconstruction loss component of VAE \\
$D_{KL}$ \> Kullback-Leibler divergence \\
$\text{KS}$ \> Kolmogorov-Smirnov statistic (distributional fidelity) \\
$\text{F1}_{min}$ \> F1-score for the minority class (primary utility metric) \\
$\text{AUC}$ \> Area Under the ROC Curve (overall utility metric) \\
$\text{DCR}$ \> Distance to Closest Record (empirical privacy metric) \\
\end{tabbing}
